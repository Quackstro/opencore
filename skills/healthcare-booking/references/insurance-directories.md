# Insurance Provider Directories

## Aetna

- **Provider Search**: https://www.aetna.com/dsepublic/#/contentPage?page=providerSearchLanding
- **Direct URL**: https://www.aetna.com/individuals-families/find-a-doctor.html
- **Tips**: Select "Open Choice PPO" as plan type, filter by "Accepting new patients"

## Blue Cross Blue Shield (Florida)

- **Provider Search**: https://www.floridablue.com/find-a-doctor
- **Network**: BlueOptions, BlueCare

## United Healthcare

- **Provider Search**: https://www.uhc.com/find-a-doctor
- **Tips**: Need to specify Choice Plus, Options PPO, etc.

## Cigna

- **Provider Search**: https://hcpdirectory.cigna.com/web/public/consumer/directory
- **Tips**: Filter by Open Access Plus if applicable

## Humana

- **Provider Search**: https://www.humana.com/finder/medical

## General Aggregators

- **Zocdoc**: https://www.zocdoc.com (online booking, insurance filter)
- **Healthgrades**: https://www.healthgrades.com
- **Vitals**: https://www.vitals.com
- **WebMD**: https://doctor.webmd.com

## Tips

1. Always verify directly with the practice that they accept the specific plan
2. PPO directories are usually larger than HMO
3. "In-network" status can change - confirm before appointment
4. Ask about "new patient" availability vs established patient slots
